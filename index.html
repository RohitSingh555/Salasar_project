SALASAR GROUP

REAL ESTATE DEVELOPMENT HOUSING / COMMERCIAL

With over 42 years of experience in the construction business, we have successfully completed numerous residential and commercial projects in Bhayander and Mira Road. The Salasar Group stands as a prominent real estate development firm, equipped with extensive expertise in development, construction, investment analysis, and various financing structures. Established in 1982, our firm's primary focus was to develop, manage, and lease projects predominantly controlled by Salasar. Over the years, we have diversified our portfolio, expanding into all sectors of the real estate industry and beyond. Our organization continues to thrive, growing in both size and diversity, while maintaining a steadfast commitment to excellence.
