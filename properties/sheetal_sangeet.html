Overview

Welcome to Salasar Sheetal Sangeet, a prestigious residential venture nestled in the serene environs of Sheetal Nagar, MTNL Road, Mira Road East. Developed by Salasar Lifespaces, this meticulously crafted project boasts a towering structure of ground plus 16 floors, offering a total of 5 flats per floor. Featuring elegantly designed 2 BHK residences spanning 603 and 664 sq ft, along with thoughtfully planned 1 BHK units covering 461 sq ft, Salasar Sheetal Sangeet promises a harmonious blend of luxury, comfort, and convenience. With its close proximity to Mira Road Station and Mira Bhayandar Road, residents enjoy seamless connectivity to key destinations, making it an ideal choice for modern living.
